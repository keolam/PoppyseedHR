Customizing HR to meet your company’s unique needs

Hi, I’m Lauren. I’m the HR Consultant you may not know you needed. I have 10+ years of experience as an HR professional working with small organizations that range from organic food startups to non-profits. I have a Certificate in Human Resource Management from San Francisco State University and I’m a SHRM Certified Professional.

I’ve spent over 15 years working very closely with Founders, CEOs, and Owners. I understand the nuanced challenges that leaders face all while trying to steer the ship with the full team in tow. A strong support system is critical for any leadership team and I love this niche that I’ve found that allows me to use my small business building skills along with my people-forward approach.

How I Work

Long Term Strategic Partnership

My client relationships are mostly long term. I form strong relationships with both leadership teams and employees through regular check-ins with CEOs, Presidents, CFOs, and Founders.

  • Strategic HR planning and execution
  • Alignment with organizational goals
  • Hot topic discussions (new laws, compliance, safety)
  • Employee relations matters
  • Serve as go-to resource for all employee HR concerns

Project-Based Services

For organizations that need focused support on specific initiatives, I offer project-based engagements tailored to your immediate needs. These targeted projects deliver measurable results while building a foundation for your HR success.

  • Recruitment and hiring campaigns
  • Employee handbook development
  • Compensation audits and strategy
  • Benefits administration setup
