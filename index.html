Customizing HR to meet your company’s unique needs

With over a decade of experience as a Human Resources professional, I specialize in supporting small organizations—from organic food startups to mission-driven nonprofits. I hold a Certificate in Human Resource Management from San Francisco State University, a BS from Boston University, and am a SHRM Certified Professional.

Throughout my 15+ years of working closely with leadership teams, I’ve developed a deep understanding of the complex dynamics leaders face while guiding their teams. My approach blends strategic HR expertise with a strong people-first mindset, offering tailored support that strengthens leadership and drives sustainable growth. I thrive in the unique space where small business development and human-centered leadership intersect.

How I Work

Long Term Strategic Partnership

Long Term Strategic Partnership

Most of my client relationships are long-term, built on mutual trust and strong engagement across all levels of the organization. I meet regularly with executive leadership; typically the CEO, President, CFO, or Founder, to ensure HR strategy is aligned with broader organizational objectives. These meetings support the execution of key initiatives, oversight of ongoing projects (e.g., compliance, performance management, engagement), and resolution of HR-related challenges.


As a dedicated partner, I provide employees with consistent HR support, allowing leadership to remain informed but not burdened by day-to-day concerns.

Project-Based Services

Project-Based Services

For organizations that need focused support on specific initiatives, I offer project-based engagements tailored to your immediate needs. These targeted projects deliver measurable results while building a foundation for your HR success.


I also collaborate with clients on short-term projects, including recruitment, employee handbooks, and benefits administration.
