My Core Values

Trust

For an HR Consultant relationship to provide true value, it must be built on a foundation of mutual trust. While establishing this takes time, I firmly believe it is essential for a successful partnership.


My primary goals are to build trust with senior leadership to develop a long-term HR strategy that aligns with your organizational goals and to cultivate trust with employees so they feel comfortable approaching me with any HR-related questions, concerns, or dilemmas.

Resourcefulness

In a small organization, where margins are crucial, budgets are tight, and the competition for top talent is fierce, resourcefulness is a non-negotiable skill. High-level execution under these constraints demands a resourceful mindset from every team member. Having lived this reality, I am uniquely equipped to support teams in developing and applying this essential skill.

Excellent Customer Service

My foundation in the hospitality industry, established early in my career, instilled in me a deep commitment to excellent customer service. This service-first mindset is what distinguishes my approach to HR. I view every interaction as an opportunity to serve a "customer," always focused on finding collaborative solutions that benefit all parties involved.

Authenticity

"Be yourself; everyone else is already taken." ~Oscar Wilde
