My Core Values

Trust

Trust

For an HR Consultant relationship to provide true value, we have to trust each other. Trust takes time to establish, but I firmly believe that a successful partnership must be built on this foundation. My goals are to build trust with senior leadership so we can work on the long-term HR strategy that aligns with the organizational goals. And also to build trust with the employees so they feel comfortable coming to me for any HR related question, concern or dilemma.

Resourcefulness

Resourcefulness

For anyone who has ever thrived in a small organization, this skill is key! Margins are crucial. Budgets are tight. Competition for top talent is fierce. Executing at a high level without deep pockets requires everyone on the team to have a resourcefulness mindset. I know this, I've lived it and now I know how to support a team or help them develop this skill.

Excellent Customer Service

Excellent Customer Service

During my early career years I worked in the hospitality industry. I believe this deep layer of customer service skills set me apart from other HR professionals. I treat everyone like a 'customer' with the goal of finding solutions that benefit both parties. I build strong relationships where we can collaborate creatively.
